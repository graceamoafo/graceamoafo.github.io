About Me, The Walking Jukebox


Grace Amoafo

With Passion All Types of Music

Ever since I was little, music has been the most significant aspect of my life. I practically came out of the womb singing! As I get older, this significance only continues to grow even more. Most of the time I have headphones in, and when I can't do that, I am singing. Music has never been something as simple as noise to me. It has always been so much more. It has the unique capability of changing my mood, either from sad to happy, or from submissive to indignant. In the midst of the choas and injustices the world has to offer, music has allowed me to both confront and escape from the harsh realities of the world. I hope that music can do the same for others too.
